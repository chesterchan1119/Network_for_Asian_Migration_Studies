About


This network aims to connect people and researchers with interest and expertise in Asian migration studies. We welcome both institutional and independent scholars to join and coordinate efforts in organizing events that enhance exchange of knowledge concerning Asian migration studies and publicize most updated research findings related to Asian migration.
