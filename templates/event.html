Events

Here is your gateway to a vibrant and collaborative space dedicated to the exploration and understanding of Asian migration. This platform brings together scholars, researchers, practitioners, and policymakers who are passionate about examining the multifaceted dimensions of migration in the Asian context. Through our events, we aim to foster dialogue, share knowledge, and promote interdisciplinary collaboration among experts from various disciplines. From insightful panel discussions to thought-provoking keynote speeches, our events provide a platform to delve into critical issues such as immigration policies, cultural integration, labor markets, and social dynamics. Join us as we engage in meaningful conversations, advance research frontiers, and strive to make a positive impact on migration studies in Asia. Together, let's build bridges of knowledge and understanding in the fascinating realm of Asian migration.
